# Limitations and safety

## Non-clinical scope

The repository is an educational historical artifact. It has not been validated for clinical use and must not be used for diagnosis, treatment, screening, triage, or patient-facing decisions.

## Reproducibility limitations

- The referenced data paths are absent from the public repository.
- Historical scripts contain local-path assumptions and may require MATLAB toolboxes not documented in the original material.
- The retained source may include legacy text encoding in comments; it is preserved for historical fidelity.
- There is no verified training/evaluation split, independent test set, calibration analysis, error analysis, or uncertainty assessment.

## Data and privacy limitations

No data-rights conclusion is implied by the presence of code or archived files. Do not infer consent, de-identification, or redistribution rights. Preserve privacy and comply with applicable data-governance requirements before working with any medical imagery.

## Reporting limitations

Do not report model metrics or compare this material with clinical systems unless a separately documented, rights-cleared, reproducible evaluation supports that statement.
